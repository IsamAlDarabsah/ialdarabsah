Overview

Research

Three strands, with a common thread: what happens to a dynamical system when the derivative is replaced by a non-local operator, and how to compute the answer reliably.

01ongoing

Fractional eco-epidemiological systems

Caputo-derivative predator–prey models carrying two competing disease strains in the prey population, with a generalised Holling response and herd-behaviour exponent $k$. The analysis covers positivity and boundedness, classification of strain-specific and coexistence equilibria, basic reproduction numbers $\mathcal{R}_{0,1}$ and $\mathcal{R}_{0,2}$, and local stability via Matignon's condition $|\arg(\lambda)| > \alpha\pi/2$. The open problem I keep returning to is the non-Lipschitz singularity at the origin when $0 < k < 1$.

Caputo derivative · Matignon condition · Mittag-Leffler decay · strain competition

Tumour–immune–drug reaction–diffusion models

A two-dimensional spatial Caputo model coupling tumour, effector immune, and drug concentrations. Work here covers nondimensionalisation, L1 time-stepping, and the qualitative behaviour of treatment protocols under memory effects.

L1 scheme · nondimensionalisation · oncology

Numerical methods for nonlinear PDEs

Laplace residual power series methods for fifth-order Kawahara-type equations with multiple quadratic nonlinearities, and sinc-collocation schemes combined with weighted shifted Grünwald differences for the time-fractional case. Root-exponential convergence, Fréchet derivatives, and Kronecker-product Jacobians in Newton iteration.

LRPSM · sinc-collocation · WSGD · Kawahara equation
